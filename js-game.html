A Javascript-based Browser Game

Published: April 16, 2023

Growing up, I spent a lot of time playing virtual online games like Ameba Pico and Ameba Pigg. One of my favorite mini-games in these games was the casino, where I always found myself gravitating towards the classic card game, blackjack. My passion for this game has inspired me to develop my own version of it for a project I am currently working on.

Blackjack is a timeless game that requires a combination of skill, strategy, and luck to win. Players aim to beat the dealer by achieving a hand value of 21, or as close to it as possible, without going over. I am excited to incorporate my own unique spin on the game while staying true to its classic rules and strategies.

Developing my own version of blackjack for this project has allowed me to channel my creativity and passion for game development. I am eager to see where this project takes me and to share my love for this game with others.

Developing this game was one hell of a journey, as I had to search for the appropriate resources and incorporate suitable sounds and images to make it engaging. The process gave me a headache, but in the end, I was able to pull it off successfully.

Undertaking the challenge of creating a game requires significant effort and dedication, particularly when it comes to sourcing the necessary resources. It can be overwhelming to navigate through the vast array of options available and find the perfect fit for your project. However, the satisfaction that comes with completing the task makes it all worth it.

Despite the challenges I encountered along the way, I am pleased with the outcome of the game. It is fulfilling to see my ideas come to life and create something that others can enjoy. The process has taught me valuable lessons in perseverance and problem-solving, which I can apply to other projects in the future.
